My name is Lucas Bautista Santiago and I am from North Carolina.
From a young age, music has held a special place in my heart. In my current job, I have to make long drives, which for most people may be unenjoyable.
However, for me, this is a time I can get lost in my music and escape.
I enjoy listening to hip hop the most, but I also enjoy sampling all kinds of music and collecting vintage vinyl.
I wanted to share with others what I am very passionate about and I hope that I can spark that passion in others.
In my free time, I like to watch futbol and game.
